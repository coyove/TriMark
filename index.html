TriMark is a simple tool which generates a special bookmarklet to help you hide some secrets:

  When someone single clicked it, he will just be guided to the dummy URL you set;
  When you triple clicked it, you will be redirected to the real URL.

If you have to place the bookmarklet into somewhere that doesn't support triple-click (e.g. Chrome's dropdown menu), try increase "Time Frame" so you can "click three times" to trigger the real URL.
